🙏🏾 When God Was Silent

There were nights I cried and heard nothing. Days I prayed and still — silence. I questioned my worth, my purpose, even my prayers.

But in that silence, something deeper was happening. God wasn’t ignoring me — He was growing me. Teaching me to trust without proof, to walk by faith and not by sight.

Now I see: silence wasn’t punishment. It was preparation. For strength, for wisdom, for purpose I didn’t even know I needed.

So if God feels silent right now — hold on. You’re not forgotten. You’re being formed.
