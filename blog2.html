Mary Goiri

💫 Becoming the Woman I Needed

I looked at the girl I used to be — scared, soft-spoken, always trying to be enough.

And I whispered: “You made it.”

I became the woman I needed. The one who sets boundaries with love. The one who speaks up. The one who forgives her past and builds her future.

Healing didn’t come fast. It came in moments — choosing me, over and over again. It came in tears, in joy, in faith.

If you’re still becoming, I see you. Keep going. She’s waiting for you. And when you meet her, hug her tight. Because she is you — free, fearless, and finally home.
