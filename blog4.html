❤ Some Days, I Don’t Feel Strong

Some days, I feel tired of being the strong one. I want to cry without explaining, to rest without guilt, to just be. I want someone to say, "You don’t have to carry it all today."

Real life isn’t always brave. Sometimes it’s silent breakdowns in the shower, or smiling through heaviness because others depend on you.

But even in those moments — when I feel weak, unsure, messy — I’m still worthy. I’m still growing. I’m still me.

This is your reminder: strength isn’t about never falling. It’s about getting back up, even if you're crawling.
