🌿 Rising from the Mud

There are moments in life when everything feels like it's sinking — like the ground beneath you is mud instead of stone.

I’ve lived those moments. I’ve cried in silence, smiled while breaking, and carried pain that didn’t have words.

But through it all, something inside me refused to stay buried. Faith. Grit. Purpose.

This post is for the woman still standing in the mud — feeling stuck, unsure, unseen. I want to tell you: You are becoming. 🌱

You are not buried. You are planted.
